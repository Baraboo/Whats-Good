          I had heard that this place is very known for it's desserts and the like, basically to satisfy the sweet tooth in you. I wouldn't blame it on the restaurant maybe, but on that particular day the desserts were a disaster. Being a home Baker myself, I totally know how at times your products tend to go wrong.
        The classic cheesecake was a disaster. The bottom layer was soggy and very watery and tasteless. The cheesecake didn't have the bottom crunch.
        The red velvet was okay, but it wasn't one of the finest I've had.
        What saved our evening were the savoury items we called for. The scallion prawns and the fish and chips were one of the finest dishes I've ever tasted.
        The scallion prawns were in a very nice clear sauce of lemon garlic and butter, with breads as accomplishments. Perfecto!
        The fish fingers of the fish and chips were nicely crumb fried and had the crunch on the outer but soft and juicy on the insides. The dip was a coconut based one, and complemented the fish fingers.

        Also, the staff are not well versed with the menu and have little troubles understanding what we called for. Initialling the waiter got us two prawn dishes and kept arguing that we ordered it. The manager came to the rescue though.

        Not too expensive but also cannot be called a budget place. Affordable much!

          Glen’s bakehouse, Indiranagar turned out to be one of the worst dining experience for us, a group of seven, who were apparently having a meet up and celebrating my own birthday. Poor me, I used to recommend Glen’s to lot of my friends just by considering their great service and food that I had at their Lavelle road outlet. This outlet near to Toit Brewpub in Indiranagar completely failed at impressing us. Upon reading the other user comments, I think there is something seriously wrong with the service here. Here is what happened.

        Saturday 8:15 PM: Me and my friend has reached and realized that there is no big table to accommodate seven people all at once. Asked the counter for help and they just kept ignoring our request by saying let the people come and then they will do something about it. I just couldn’t stand it because it was Saturday night and people were pouring in. And to add up, Glen’s Bakehouse don’t accept reservations.

        8:20 PM: We found a square table outside, on the left side of the restaurant, and we occupied it waiting for the neighboring table to get free. We asked to move this table just 20m to merge with one of the round table outside and they rejected it saying “That is not possible!”

        8:30 PM: Friends started coming in and we were running out of chair and table. Two girls came in and asked for the table we were waiting and the waiter instantly nodded YES to it. I got really angry at the waiter and we have to interrupt that deal our self.

        8:40 PM: Friends were still standing as there was no chair to sit. Fed up with the waiter’s response one of us went inside and grabbed chairs.

        9:00 PM: Done with cake cutting. FYI: We bought the cake from there itself. Still no one cared to give us a glass of water which is a normal courtesy in any restaurant. We placed our order and still no sign of water, which we have asked half an hour before. GETTING WAITERS ATTENTION IS A BIG TASK!

        9:05 PM: We placed order for drinks and got delivered in matter of minutes, I appreciate that.

        9:40 PM: After half an hour the Veg pizzas got delivered leaving the Non-veg people starving.

        10:00 PM: Finally everything got served and we literally have to save up on our drinks as there is no drinking water. We didn’t want to create a scene there; in fact we were really busy chit chatting.

        The waiters seemed so arrogant and the next table people were also fighting for something. Finally one guy came with a jug of water and realized there is no glass to pour the water. He went inside and grabbed four glasses of water for seven people and said he will be back. That’s it! He never showed up until it’s time to pay the bill.

        11:30PM: We left the place. NEVER EVER HOST A PARTY AT THIS PLACE!

        Glen’s bakehouse in Indiranagar is a perfect example on how to serve the customers, note the sarcasm. I loved the food; the pizzas were delicious and mouth-watering. To the management: Why guys? It’s a big place and there are lots of tables to serve. The waiters are completely out numbered just by considering the number of tables. At least try to put 2 big tables considering the party occasions like this. I understand it’s our mistake to select a café which doesn’t have proper seating to accommodate a large group but, the attitude of the people who runs the place is unspeakable. No one should have an experience that we had to go through on a birthday party. Thanks for ruining my BIRTHDAY PARTY! I want someone from the management to respond to this.

          Lively ambiance, ample seating area, a decent food spread, quick service and wallet-friendly; that's Glen's Bakehouse for you.

        My friends repeatedly complained about the unresponsive staff here. But my experience was quite the opposite. Food was served within less than 10 minutes. The place is well staffed and customers promptly catered to from my observation.

        Not in a mood to experiment, I went for the conventional, safe choices - cold coffee and cheesy alfredo chicken pasta. I didn't quite have the appetite to try the much renowned desserts though I would recommend them as per hearsay.

        I had the unfortunate, embarrassing experience of having forgotten my wallet which, as predicted, I realised after polishing off my meal. The Glen's folks were kind enough to not create a scene and let me leave with the promise of paying later (which I did); extra points for that.

        So yeah, Glen's bakehouse is a good choice for the tried and tested Italian cuisine without burning a hole through your pockets.
