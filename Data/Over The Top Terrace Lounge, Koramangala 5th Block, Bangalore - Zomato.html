          I visited this place few months back with 4 of my friends. We decided to try this place out on a friends’ recommendation. We came here on a Sunday and so we got our sets immediately. As the name say, it was on the roof top, luckily they have shades above since it was raining that evening.

        We started with the Veg Nachos and some soups of our choice. The nachos did look appealing and the taste lived up to the looks. Since I had the hot and sour to myself, I remember the taste. It wasn’t bad but not much good. Moving on to the main course, one of ordered a burger, me a pasta and the others had a Punjabi subzi with roti. To be honest, amongst these 3 items only the subzi was good. In fact Delicious. The Pasta bowl was deceiving, when the waiter was carrying the food to our table I was so excited to have the amount of pasta, looking at the size of the serving plate, but I was utterly sad looking at the quantity that was actually being served. Priced at 250 rs the quantity which was served was surprising. The burger lacked the moisture.

        We also had few mocktails and cocktails all of which were satisfactory.
        Long Island Ice Tea and Exotic Enigma to name.

        To sum it up, it wasn’t an unpleasant experience, but there’s lots of scope to improvise.
