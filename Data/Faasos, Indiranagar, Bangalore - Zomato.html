          Ordered omelette pav sausage combo and cheese melt chicken wrap.

        Delivery was pretty fast.

        After reading the good reviews, I was expecting a lot in terms of quality.

        The packaging was decent. But as I opened the package, the smell of stinking food filled the atmosphere. The omelette smelt like rotten eggs. Neither did I liked the sausages. Were they made out of soya bean instead of chicken bits? I had a horrible experience.

        The chicken cheese melt wrap was better in comparison. Didn't stink at least. And there was an adequate amount of cheese in the wrap. However, it could have been a lot better. When you say wrap, you should not wrap a roti around the filling. Wrap is different.

        I don't know how some people liked the food but I was disappointed with the food. Won't buy again from this outlet.
