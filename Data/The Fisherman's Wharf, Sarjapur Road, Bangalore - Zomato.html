          Went here on Sunday for dinner. As expected very less number of people. We took a seat at the starting place.

        Ambience: I would term it to be good. Music was good too. But there were lot of mosquito in the place. That was a big turn off even though they provided mosquito coils.

        Food: Am not much of a seafood lover. But I liked the rawa pomfret and the prawns. They had some good goan dish on menu. But not being in a mood to experiment went ahead with regular chicken. Goan style chicken was good. I liked the idea of giving coke with pudina and calling it midnight Bangalore or something like that. You can try that out. It was good.

        Service: Perfect. The servings were fast. Did not have to wait much. Might be possible due to less people.

        Overall: A must visit if you are nearby. They have an awesome lunch buffet is what I have heard. Good variety of sea food if you are sea food lover. Must go.
