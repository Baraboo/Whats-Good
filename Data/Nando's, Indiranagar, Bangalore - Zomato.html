          Nando's has always been on my wishlist ever since it arrived to Bangalore.Having heard so much about it from around the world, I was really looking forward for a good meal.

        Located in Indiranagar, it's not difficult to spot. Entering the place you are welcomed by a large seating area and overall this place has pretty good ambience.

        The way you place an order is slightly different.You first choose what you want from the menu and then go up to one of the counters and place your order and pay for the food there itself,you are then given a number which you place on your table, the food will then be brought to you once it's prepared.

        We ordered the 1/2 peri peri chicken with hot sauce , the veg espetada and the mushroom, halloumi and chilli jam burger.The food was really good and the flavours were definitely not something you would sample everyday.The veg espetada was a skewer of paneer, mushroom and other veg options and had an underlining peri peri flavour and was really good.The dish of the day for me was the peri peri chicken with that amazing hot sauce.In my opinion, the "hot" hot sauce was really delicious compared to the "mild" hot sauce but was still quite good nevertheless.The burger was nice, which was served with fries, but a bit pricey in my opinion.All dishes came with an option of one or two sides like coleslaw, chips,wedges,corn etc and really helped balance out the main dishes.

        All in all Nando's I feel is a must visit for any foodie who wants to try out new flavours and especially for anyone who loves chicken.One thing I forgot to mention is that you get unlimited refills on beverages if you opt for it.Loved the peri peri chicken and will definitely be back for more.
