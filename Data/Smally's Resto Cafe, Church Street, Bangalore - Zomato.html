        Rated
          Smally’s might be small but they have a fantastically big heart with their waiters and hosts, extremely pleasant company to hang out with.

        You are welcomed by their rustic, small little door and find yourself in a colourful and trendy cafe with hippie posters and comic-like paintings that make for a good read while you’re waiting for your food.

        Coming to their food; regardless of which branch you frequent; the Trucker burger is a fast-seller and one of their most popular dishes. It is a double patty whopper with well-done steak and is perhaps one of the more juicier burgers in town!
        An accompaniment; the Smally’s Brownie Shake was extremely delicious and I loved the fact that one could bite into tiny crumbles of brownie from it.

        What did surprise us about the menu was along with the American and Continental style dishes there was a dash of the Mangalorean cuisine with Ghee Roast and Mangalorean Chicken Curry. Gotta give that a try on my next visit.

        So all in all, a small, happy, fun (not to mention, affordable), and delicious world invites you at Smallys.
