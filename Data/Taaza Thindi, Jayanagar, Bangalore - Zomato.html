          First thing that strikes one when you visit this place is the way everything is organized and how the crowd automatically follow everything in spite of it being crowded.
        Prices are so reasonable, menu is simple with very few items - idli, vade, masala dosa, plain dosa, khara bath, kesari bath and coffee.
        Another thing is they don't serve sambar, it is only chutney and they serve in loads and there is a separate chutney section.
        Eat as much as you can. Enjoy. Everything tastes well.
        Felt like I was in Brahmins cafe.

        Rated
          I have been planning to visit this place since two years and finally, I get the chance to be here. There was nothing on which I could say that this certain thing I didn't like about this place, or this certain could be improved. I had masala dosas, idlis, khara bhaat, and filter coffee. Masala dosa was thick yet super crispy and they pour a spoon of hot ghee on dosas with fresh mint coconut chutney. Being a North Indian it is quite impossible for me to have idlis without sambhar, here they serve idlis in coconut chutney. Idlis were really really soft, I haven't had such soft idlis ever before. Khara bhaat was as same as my mother cooks namkeen daliya at home.
        Here the coupon system works so one has to stand in lines for their food, but even then it was such a quiet and well-managed place. Every staff member has cap and face mask, the place was really neat, every corner was neat and tidy. They were serving food in well-sterilised plates. And the best part is they only pack in your own steel boxes, and don't use any plastic packaging material. Yet because of this, I wasn't able to pack for my friends but this is the best thing they can do.
        If you are in Bangalore even for a day, you must visit this place. Highly recommending this place :)

          If there was a rating higher than 5 that I could give to Taaza Thindi, I most certainly would!! This place serves the perfect south Indian breakfast.
