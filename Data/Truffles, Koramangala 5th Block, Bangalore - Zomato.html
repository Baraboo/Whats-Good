          Waiting outside Truffles for 30-40 minutes for a table is a very common thing. That's how popular this place is! Almost every other person would recommend this place to you if you are new in Bangalore. Their signature dish is equally popular as the the place itself-the famous All American cheese burger. They serve kickass burgers at reasonable prices, making it a renowned place among the youth.

        I have visited this place multiple times and always had a pleasant experience. For the first few visits my order was the same- All American cheese burger either lamb or chicken with ferrero rocher shake. The first time I had this burger it felt heavenly. The burger has an additional cheese patty. And with every bite your mouth explodes with cheese! The other burgers are also good. I've tried the sloppy joe, cheese burst lamb burger and the tandoori burger. They are good but nothing beats the All American cheese burger! Even writing about this burger makes me want to go and have one.

        Apart from burgers they have a lot of other stuff on their menu. They have delicious appetisers like fried mozzarella sticks, Peri peri prawns and cheesy mex fries. The pesto pasta here is also tasty. The beautiful flavour of pesto comes out really well. The chicken lasagna isn't very nice. Coming to the shakes and dessert, the red velvet sludge, Ferrero rocher shake and cake fudge, kit Kat cake fudge and very vanilla are some of the delectable items on their menu.

        Overall a great place to hang out and very pocket friendly. Delicious food at reasonable prices. Service and ambiance is decent. A must visit place in Bangalore.

          Truffles is one place I was really excited to visit. Raving reviews, amazing ratings and word of mouth- Truffles was a mandatory food joint recommendation that every friend of mine has been making ever since I came to Bangalore. Before I write this review, I must make it clear that I had tremendous expectations from the place. So when I stepped out of the cab infront of Truffles I was excited to the point of giddy.

        I am not a fan of overtly crowded places and though Truffles looks amazing on the outside(with sprinklers fitted at the top showering you with a pleasant light drizzle as you enter), it is way too crowded and looks like a brightly lit bland office cafeteria with a constant cacophony.

        The place has been designed for accommodating maximum customers owing to the immense popularity of the place. We ordered the Truffles' famous All American Cheese Burger, a Truffles Rooster burger, Ferrero Rocher shake and Green Apple mojito. And the regular burgers were humongous and tremendously filling without compromising on the taste. A burger plate comes with chips on the sides and is enough to satiate your cravings. The shake was equally good though we could hardly gobble down anything after the burgers. The green apple mojito was refreshing and light and something I would recommend ordering if you go for something as heavy as a Truffles burger.

        But then I must acknowledge the fact that many restaurants in Bangalore serve equally good food as Truffles(and some even better) and the immense popularity of the place could be attributed to the fact that Truffles is easy on the pocket and succeeds gloriously in satiating your hunger pangs. The staff is prompt and the crowd is young and merry.

        Truffles is a must visit mainly because it is so highly recommended and such a popular destination for foodies in Bangalore.
