          Utter disappointment right from the beginning!

        The place was cramped with hardly any space to move between tables. The ambience was quite dull and not at all airy. We were guided to our chair promptly but had to ask a waiter to start serving ( unlike other buffet spreads where they ask your preference veg/nonveg, spice levels etc before service) we also had to remind them to even get us bottle water.

        Coming to the spread: the Zomato menu boasted of exotic meat being served which was Missing. they get kept getting veg starters even after specifying to bring in non veg items. The chilly garlic prawns were smelly and stale much to our dismay. The fish fry was average. The chicken kabab was too spicy for our level and we felt bad to waste the food. Our table never saw the mutton seekh kabab!!

        The live pasta counter made us pasta with mushroom and white sauce which was again average. Pani Puri was ok. The live Asian counter had no exotic meats.

        We were so dissatisfied we couldn't even look at the main course.

        Desserts: kheer, gulab jamuns were good. Didn't find any of the other desserts attractive.

        After having seen and tasted better buffet spreads in Bangalore, this place is a big thumbs down! We were too dissatisfied to even take pics!

        Lesson learnt: never go by the Zomato ratings blindly!
