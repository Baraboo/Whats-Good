          I've been to Toit many times earlier but never got around to writing a review. Toit is a place where there's a buzz any day of the week with hardly a place to sit on weekends. Their in-house brewed beers are said to have maintained their quality through all these years. We went there with visitors a few days back. We ordered -
        Kerala beef fry - Surprisingly good with the typical Kerala flavors coming through and the beef cooked pretty well.
        Paprika pizza - Toit's pizzas are pretty popular and this one did not disappoint us with a nice thin and crunchy (but not like a biscuit) crust and a generous helping of toppings.
        BBQ chicken pizza - Small and well cooked pieces of chicken, however pretty less in number. Overall flavor was good with a little bit of smokiness.
        Pepperoni pizza - Excellent pizza. The crust, cheese and pepperoni were all great!
        Toit baked Nachos - Pretty decent. I would have preferred a little more of the melted cheese though.
        Chicken wings - Toit has never gone wrong with their wings and this time it didn't disappoint as well. Perfect size, well cooked with an adequate amount of BBQ sauce.
        Spicy grilled fish - This has been my all time favorite at Toit. I always order this dish. Always! The spices on the fish are brilliant and spicy but without the rawness of the spice. They use Basa although I wish they used a different fish.

        All in all a good place to hang out, have good food over some in-house beers. The wooden interiors gives it an interesting atmosphere.

        Service continues to be a negative here over all these years.

          Hey guys! Want to spend an evening well and feel awesome?!  Head straight to TOIT! 
        This is an example of how excellent Brewery/pubs should be.. The ambiance is breath-taking, service excellent, food and drinks are all available and yum! Add to that a genuine cost and you get full 5 /5 :) 
        A  friend had visited Bangalore and a small reunion of a few friends was planned. One of us suggested to go for Toit as some of her friends had highly recommended the place. Well, it was worth it..
        There are separate sections and each has different themes. There are separate and spacious spaces for smoking and non smoking sections.  I loved a small balcony for smokers in the non-smoking section too ;) It gets packed on a weekday, I can't imagine how long would one have to wait on a weekend. Its better to do reservations or be ready to wait.
        Music- neither too loud nor too light, perfect!
        Drinks- They had all the house beer and more. Their bar is always full and you will get the choice of your drink... and the beer here is to die for ..! No, I am serious.. for one- they have well described on the menu..Take your pick... We settled for a sampler first and then Blonde Ale and then Bacardi Black. Every sip of the beer was heavenly!
        Food- Excellent. Everything that we ordered was like it should be hot, pipping and correct spices... and we ordered a lot...from barbeque sauce chicken wings to Fish fingers to Fried Calamari to Bruschetta to Grilled cottage cheese!! Everything was so yummy and mouth watering... Fish was soft and melted in the mouth, chicken wings were succulent, Calamari was crispy and flavourful, Bruscheta was amazingly prepared!
         Finally, one place that had everything bang on! Add to that a company of old school buddies and you have heaven on earth!!
        The best part is .. its not that heavy on the pocket. 
        Friends and countrymen, this is THE PLACE to go if you are a foodie and want to have some genuinely good drinks and food with ambiance! 
        Kudos to the management to run the place so efficiently!
        Am I going back..? Hell Yeah!!!! :)
