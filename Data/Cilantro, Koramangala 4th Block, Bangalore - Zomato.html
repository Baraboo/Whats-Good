          In search of a good breakfast buffet headed to Cilantro. Had been passing this place on my way to office, wondering how it would be. The place looks good as you enter. Good greenery greets you.  The fare was good for the cost. (its Rs 300 less taxes). The place presents a neglected and untidy look (not dusted well, chair cushion falling off). The spread has some variety with a dish for the day (Puri Sabzi) and some items from live counter. Mr Jaykumar, the Captain seemed active, running around to see all is well. Staff is friendly. The menu had Bacon and Ham, but the kitchen was not able to provide it. It took about 10 minutes for them to inform the same. An order for double fried eggs ended up as omelette. Vadas were little hard. But then one wonders, "Itne me, itna hi milega".  But the place certainly has potential to get better, even at a higher cost.

          Summary: Awesome Breakfast Buffet

        We went to this  
        awesome place on our way back from a photography session at a lake off sarjapura.

        Order : The Breakfast Buffet

        Food (5/5) : The breakfast here is lip smacking; the buffet comprises of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes and has south Indian, continental and English; all included in one. Bakes beans, Buttered Vegetables, Idli-sambar, Upma, Vada, Veg cutlets all available to choose from with a variety of chutneys. On this particular day, the semiya Upma tasted awesome ! 

        Along with this freshly made Appams and stew and made to order masala dosas. Any type of egg dish can be ordered at the counter(Cheese , Toast and omletter, Ham and Omletter, Spanish Omlette ..whatever you want !!). We ordered the nice cheesy omletter with toast. Along with this French toast with Maple syrup, Pancakes with maple syrup and waffles with Maple syrup. All are well made and tasted especially awesome and are highly recommended. 

        Fresh cut fruits are also available along with assortment of breads can be had with fruit juice or butter milk. For the health conscious, oats &  museli with warm Milk. To end it all, a cup of sweet yoghurt. A perfect breakfast really. :)

        Ensure to get a early morning workout before coming here ! 

        HIghly Recommended : Waffles with Maple Syrup

        Ambience (4.5/5) : Casual Dining ambience with some light music in the background 

        Service (4.5/5) : Service was fast. However note that outside food items are not allowed; so we had to order the cake separate.

        Value for Money (4.5/5) : Really economical breakfast. Totally worth it

        Visit again : yes
