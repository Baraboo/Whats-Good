        The ambience is what set this place apart, in my opinion. It is located on 100ft road and is a roof top brewery. I must add, however, that they basically let the night sky do its job and haven't added anything extra to amplify the ambience.

        We ordered two cocktails and a beer. "Bullfrog" was strong and good-would recommend. "Sex on the beach" was quite sweet and tasted more like a mock tail. They have their own beer which they brew out here and we decided to go with the Vapour Ale. Must say it was different from the usual ones and worth a try.

        For the food, we got a chicken lasagne-was decent. The Spanish Fish was very fresh and came with a side of well seasoned vegetables and mashed potato (which was bland and the only downside to this dish).

        I did feel the rates are a tad over- but well, you're in a brewery in Indiranagar.. I don't think you could expect anything different.

        I have 2 reasons to deduct 2 stars from the rating. When a place charges this much, they should really take notice and maintain everything in the restaurant. Their restrooms are terrible and absolutely unclean!!!i cannot stress this enough. After washing hands there, I was pretty sure I walked out with more germs than I went in with a Secondly, the waiting staff is quite the non friendly type and we got the cold shoulder when we asked for water. I don't know if this is the general theme or whether we were unlucky with our waiter.
