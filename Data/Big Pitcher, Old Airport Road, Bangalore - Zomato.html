        Rated
          One stop destination for all awesomeness. And I really mean it. They have 6 floors as you know, and the theme's different in all of them. Which is pretty kool coz u can come here on different occassions , in different moods and not get bored. This time i tried the SplitWood. Its on the second floor.

        This place serves the signature dishes mostly , so if you are one hungry fella like me, come to hog here.

        My absolute favorite of the day would be the fish in splitwood water and murgh dum tikka splitwood.

        the sriracha sauce was pure magic . Infused in the fried fish it had flavours that you make you go gaga. Order this first, order this again and order this even after your dessert and you will still crave for more. Parcel it too may be. The murgh on the other hand came in tough competition with being marinated with pureed almond cream cheese on a tandoor. Did u read that? Almond cream cheese! ... good lord ! that was something !

        Dorei wali gosht seekh was lil better than the usual standard affair. the content was creamier /softer than usual, relished it happily ! Perking roast chicken could be worked upon. Was a lil too dry and the marination didnt peek into its core.

        Okay now lets the veggies have their glory too. Dum dum mushroom take a bow. The creamy cheese filling accentuated the dish , top notch affair! ... i dont fancy a lot of mushroom items as they dont really stand out. This did and how!!!!

        ...and , there was this chinese sorta made vegetable drum sticks. I guess it had been ages i had anything resembling chinese, and this was just fab! .. the gingery saucy tangy on the crispy roasted vegetable balls , was tasty af. Came in scoopy , and we devoured in one go! . If you are a veggie, dont miss out on the above two. If you are a non vegetarian with a bigger tummy, same goes for you.

        Golden fried baby corn was standard affair. the soya sauce that came along complimented really well. Malai seekh kebab was a one bite affair, since i had just after the non veg version of the same.

        Main course was biryani. Veg and non veg both. Veg was more flavorful. The nonveg had handsome meat in it , but the aroma of the veg was noticable.

        Desserts now. I will keep it short. Walnut praline mousse was passable. badam and kubani ka halwa was superb, elegantly made , medium sweetish and sizable . I personally loved the hot choclate brownie with coconut crusted ice cream, loved it.

        Oh, i also had a prawn dish. it had fried noodle wrap on it, The prawn was sizable and meaty. Good grub.

        Now that i am done with the food. Let me walk you through the drinks. Its imperative for me that you love the cocktails. I am not a fan of cocktails, but the drinks served here, was awesome. Super awesome.

        1. Devils in a bottle pitcher - it had a mild flaming undertone, pineapple and mint infused beautifully.

        2. Flaming ferrari- try it for the fun of it, you have it in one shot and the hype is all so exciting, and no its not scary as it seems. you are in safe hands and it also has a very decent alcohol for the entire shot quantity that you gulp. Again, try it for the fun of it, its kool.

        3. Wet Lips- rum and whipped cream infused sorta. Good.

        4. kiss on the lips - suprisingly very tasty. chocolate and vodka , was one interesting drink.

        5. high seas adventure- my personal favourite . this came in a matka . Vodka coconut milk vanilla elderflower. Top notch !

        6. romantic martini - again a good one.

        i had 7 to 8 more drinks, but since i dont remember it i guess the above 6 won over. If u like the above recommendations, lemme know, i will list out the other as well.

        If u have been here countless time before for the dance floor and the pub grub, splitwood will surprise you, with heavy hearty food and family service. Oh that reminds me, we were very messy that day, spilling drink and almost toppling the server with food ( not me, my friends, but still ..) and the servers there almost took the blame on them.

        Will i visit again? Ofcourse. a different floor but..

        Here goes my rating-

        Food- 4.5/5

        Ambience- 4/5

        Service- 4.8/5
