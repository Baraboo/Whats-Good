Next came the waffles with ice-cream and served with Maple Syrup.. The waffles were baked perfectly, the syrup was ecstatic and the Ice-cream just enhanced the experience for me as I was having it for the first time... We had ordered an omelette called Say Cheese... it had 3 kinds of cheese as a filling in it and was a treat to our taste buds... The dish named sunday morning was almost like a Lasagne.. It had baked eggs, lots of veggies and cheese again.... Going to ambience... its a completely homely feeling but I would prefer if you can get a outside seating and a ground floor seating... its the best.. Overall its a place which everyone should visit atleast once and should try one of the exotic dishes they serve.

          I was able to finally visit this place after a long time spent in fantasizing about it. This was the last place I visited in Bangalore before I left the city. I will miss the city badly.
        The ambience is the best among Cafes that I have visited so far. It has both outdoor and indoor seating. I took a seat outside. The chairs and tables might look old and rickety but still they all lend tremendously to the Cafe setup.
        I ordered the Porky Hole 9 Yards Hash. It was served along with two slices of toasted brown bread and Amul butter. The plate of pork was full and every single bite tingled my taste buds. The hash browns were perfectly crispy, the pork sausages were peppery and the pepperoni was Smoky. Simply perfect.
        I felt that the bacon pieces were very less and small. One has to search through the plate to find them. I, being a Bacon lover, was disappointed with bacon portions in the meal.
        The sauteed onions, capsicums and mushrooms greatly accentuated the entire breakfast. Even the brown bread tasted heavenly and utterly butterly delicious. 😋
        The water that they served tasted a bit funny. So go for bottled water if needed.
        The fried eggs on top was hot and really soft with the yolk evenly spreading over the dish on being cut.
        I even had some more space for a dessert as I was having a Brunch and not a breakfast. I wanted to try out their waffles. I love chocolate chips so I ordered plain waffles topped with only Choco chips. This was a mistake as I felt that to know which topping suits waffles best, one must have much more experience with waffles. If you are going for waffles for the first time then choose the existing options and don't go for the customization.
        The maple syrup served with the hot waffles was just perfect. Not too sweet and not too bland - perfectly in between the two.
        This is one Cafe that no one should miss out in Bangalore. Please do visit the place as soon as possible. A wholesome experience. Tax is only 5% which is pretty cool for the service and food that they provide.
        Pick of the Meal - Pork Sausages
        Cost - 310(Porky 9 Yards) + 100(Plain Waffles) + 50 (Choco Chips) = 460 + 23(5% Service tax). 483.
