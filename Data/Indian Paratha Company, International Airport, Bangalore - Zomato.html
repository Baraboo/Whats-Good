          If you wish to drive past the Bangalore airport and want to have some good brunch in peace, this is possibly a good bet.

        WARNING - non vegetarians, stop reading this review now. This place is pure veg and yes, I did go there.

        Ambience is classy. Located aside some rock like structures on the highway, place ain't hard to miss.
        Be ready to wait a while though cos the mornings are pretty crowded with a lot of biker clubs and cyclists.

        Waiting period is a tad bit long.

        Service is just about average , not that great though.
        The severs keep running helter skelter to serve and its possibly difficult for anyone to manage master crowds.
        Seating is limited as well. Possibly it was the time of the day when there was ample crowd.

        Food - had the pakora platter, plus all kinda parathas.
        Stuff is smooth and yum.
        The curds or the yoghurt served along with the parathas is really thick and filling.
        Side servings of pickles and chutney was super awesome

        Went with the intent of breakfast, ended up having brunch.

        Tea ☕ - want to specifically mention the masala chai. I think the best we have had ever out of home.
        Classic tea in dhaba styled glasses.

        Overall would give this place an above average rating. Will go back again.
